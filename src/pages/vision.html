Our Vision

Grace of God Ministry Leicester

Help People in Need

One of our visions for Grace of God Ministry, is helping those in need, providing those who need food and clothing. We have already started this vision of ours, by gifting food, clothing and Bibles. Learning from the Bible and learning about the word of God is important as God has told us to help those in need and teach them about God and his works. We wish to continue with the vision and proceed with helping those in need.

"Whoever oppresses the poor shows contempt for their Maker, but whoever is kind to the needy honors God. Proverbs 14:31

Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap. For with the measure you use, it will be measured to you.” Luke 6:38

Build a Church

Our second vision for Grace of God Ministry is to build few churches in different locations around the world, where there are no churches. We plan to proceed with this vision,so that the people can visit and listen to the word of God. We hope to preach the word of God to as many people as we can, so that their lives too can be transformed, just like how God changed ours, after we started attending Church. Church is a place of worship, where all those who are a part of Grace of God Ministry, can come together as a community to praise and glorify God’s name. Together we can become a stronger community of Grace of God Ministry.

24“Therefore everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock. 25 The rain came down, the streams rose, and the winds blew and beat against that house; yet it did not fall, because it had its foundation on the rock. 26 But everyone who hears these words of mine and does not put them into practice is like a foolish man who built his house on sand. 27 The rain came down, the streams rose, and the winds blew and beat against that house, and it fell with a great crash.” Matthew 7:24-27

Deliver the gospal of god.

Our third vision for Grace of God Ministry is to deliver the Gospel of God to everyone who is in the darkness, and show them the light through Jesus Christ.

Prepare pastors and servants of the lord

Our fourth vision for Grace of God Ministry, is to prepare the youngsters and overall the people in our Ministry, for them to be servants of the Lord. Those who obey God’s Commandments written in the Bible and practice them, are servants of the Lord. Our aim is to prepare all those who are a part of our ministry to learn and become servants of the Lord. We, people of God serve a righteous master,and to be the servants of God, we must learn to be a praying person, humble and be a faithful servant to God. God has given each and everyone of his people spiritual gifts. We need to receive those gifts, and use them as God has directed us to, and save those from sins.
