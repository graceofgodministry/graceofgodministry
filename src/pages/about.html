Meet the three main trustee of Grace of God.

Mohanlal Bava

I am motivated to help those who are lost, by showing them and leading them into the right path, through Jesus Christ. I am excited to hear their testimonies and miracles Jesus Christ has done for them, and how he sacrificed his life for them. Spreading the word of God and baptising them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit is my goal, as it is written in Matthew 28:19. God already knows you by name, and he has chosen you from the beginning. He knocks on the door of your heart, and he wants you to let him in.

Vice President - Grace of God

Dilip Manikant

As president of Grace of God Ministry, I want to use my position to reach out to many people who have never heard the word of God before through this ministry. To follow the word of God, my ambition is to preach the word of God in areas that are poor, and teach them about the sacrifice that Jesus paid for their sins. I would like to give to those who are in need with clothing, food, and hand out Bibles for them to read. My aspiration for the future is to help young children who are unable to afford an education for themselves, and provide them with this opportunity. Also, I hope to support elderly and orphanage children, and provide for their needs.

President - Grace of God

Lalgi Mulgi

As a Trustee of the Grace of God Ministry, I would like to lead those who are in the darkness and direct them into the light through the word of God. I hope to do this by preaching the word of God in areas that have never heard the word of God before. Educating those who are unable to read or write is a goal of mine through this ministry, and to also fulfill the needs of those who do not have the necessities they need.

About Us

Grace of God Ministry is an organisation working in different countries, preaching and teaching those who have never heard the word of God.

Our vision is to save as many people from the darkness and show them the light through Jesus Christ.
